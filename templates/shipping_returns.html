Shipping & Replacements

We want you to have a smooth and happy experience with Lush Lilac! Please read our shipping and replacement policy below.

Shipping

  • Orders are shipped within 2-3 business days after payment confirmation.
  • We ship across India via trusted courier partners.
  • You will receive a tracking link by email once your order is dispatched.

Replacement Policy

  • We do not offer returns at this time.
  • We offer free replacement only in the following cases:
    • Product received is damaged in transit
    • You received the wrong product
    • Wrong size or color sent (if applicable)
  • To request a replacement, please email us at lushlilac30@gmail.com within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the product and packaging.
  • We do not offer replacements for change of mind or incorrect address provided.

Need Help?

If you have any questions about your order, shipping, or our replacement policy, please contact us at lushlilac30@gmail.com.
